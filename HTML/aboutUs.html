Get To Know Us Better!

Our Mission

Choose-hope aims to help disconnected young people discover the greatness within, by supporting and providing opportunities to encourage and facilitate positive life and to keep them off the streets.

Our Vission

We strongly believe that the youth of the country should have every opportunity to live the life they aspire to, and build a future they choose. Choose-hope envisions an Australia where all of us have a safe home and can thrive.

Our Services

At choose-hope, we offer access to a wide range of free services that are specifically designed for the youth of Australia. Which includes: Reconnect programs, Social Housing, Health Care, Job Opportunities, Legal Advice, and Laundry facilities. These are aimed to facilitate and establish a path for Better Life, Better Future!

Our People

The team behind the project initiative is at the center of everything we do. Each plays their special part, applying their unique skills to the pursuit of one common goal: to prevent youth homelessness and ensure people and communities in need can thrive.
